Rename your own copy of the birthday BGM to exactly:

    theme.mp3

...and place it in this same "public" folder, right next to this file.

The site automatically looks for /theme.mp3 at runtime and plays it once
the birthday page loads. If it's missing, the site falls back to a short
original instrumental so it's never silent — but your own file will
always be used first if it's here.

(This project does not ship with any copyrighted audio bundled in — you
need to add your own legally-owned file.)
